About Me

With a background rooted in Counter-Strike, I've been immersed in the gaming world since a young age, honing my skills through years of practice and dedication. Over the past 4 years, I've had the pleasure of coaching and assisting students and clients of all levels, witnessing their growth and success. My coaching philosophy revolves around the belief that anyone can become a great gamer with the right mindset, attitude, and training. I strive to instill these values in my students, helping them develop the skills and confidence necessary to succeed in any game they play. So if you're looking to take your gaming skills to the next level, look no further than me "Domestico". Let's work together to achieve your gaming goals and become the best gamer you can be!

What Can I Do For You?

Here are some of the services that I offer when it comes to coaching.

One-on-one

One-on-one sessions are more than just training; they're a partnership aimed at elevating your play to competitive ranks. Equip yourself with the knowledge and skills to not just play, but to outplay.

Esports Mentorship

Navigate the competitive world of esports with a mentor who understands the industry. From improving your in-game skills to providing guidance on team dynamics and personal branding, I can help you become a well-rounded competitive player.

Strategy Sessions

Dive deep into the tactical aspects of gaming with focused strategy sessions. We'll cover map analysis, agent selection, and team play strategies to give you an edge over the competition.
